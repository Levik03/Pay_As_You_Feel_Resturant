F.E.A.S.T (Food,Enjoyment,Appreciation,Sharing,Trust) is a nonprofit organization to help combat food waste created by the local major supermarkets and distributors in their area. Our goal is to divert food waste into the Resturant where people can pay if they can, and if they are unable, they can still get a meal of nourishing food for themselves and their family.
